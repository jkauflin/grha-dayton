    Gander Road Homeowners Association

    You have found the web site of the Gander Road Homeowners Association. Our association is a combination of the subdivisions of Quail Hollow and Willow Glen, located in the northeast corner of Dayton, OH. This site is intended primarily as a means of communication between the Board of Trustees and the homeowners of the association.

    Treasurer info for Realtors and Title Agents

    All questions regarding the status of dues payments and/or liens on a particular property should be directed to our Treasurer, Misty Wilker, (937) 554-9414, email: treasurer@grha-dayton.org

    Email contact is preferred and usually the best way to get her. Like most of the board, she has a full time day job, so don't expect an answer immediately.

    Click Contact Us on the top menu for the contact information of the entire GRHA Board of Trustees.

    Monthly Trustee meetings

    The GRHA Board of Trustees meets the 2nd Thursday of every month at 7:00pm at the city of Dayton Fire Station #12 on Brandt Pike (just south of Needmore). This meeting is open to all members of the GRHA.

    (Click here for map to Fire Station #12)

    Meeting Minutes can be found in the Quail Call newsletter

    *** Due to COVID-19, Public Meetings have been cancelled for the time being ***

    The Board will continue to meet virtually and post messages on this website and Facebook

    Events / Calendar

    The GRHA hosts the following events during the year. In addition we sometimes schedule volunteer events for cleanup or special projects.
    Event Day
    Easter Event Saturday, usually the week before Easter
    Neighborhood Garage Sale Saturday, usually 2nd week in May
    Annual Meeting Wednesday, 3rd week in September
    Halloween Event Saturday, usually the week before Halloween
    Halloween Donut Stations Halloween Day
    Christmas Decorating Contest Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, usually 2nd week in December
