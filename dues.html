Dues Help Information

Enter your address (or just house number) in the box that says Property Address, and hit Enter or click Search
Click on the green "Dues Statement" button for your property to check your outstanding amount
The PayPal button will be on the statement for you to pay online as long as the only outstanding amount is the current year's dues. You do not need a PayPal account to use the online payment service but the GRHA adds a $4.00 service fee to cover processing fees from Paypal.
IF you have liens and/or past due assessments:
Please contact the treasurer at treasurer@grha-dayton.org for a PayPal invoice if you would like to pay outstanding dues online, however there is a processing fee added by PayPal for this special invoice
Contact the treasurer to halt additional interest for accruing if you will be mailing in a check for payment (payment amount is good for 30 days, then interest begins to accrue again)
